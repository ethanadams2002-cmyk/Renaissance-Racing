About

Renaissance Racing is a competitive motorsports team founded by Ethan Adams, a lifelong racer and master technician. We compete in motorcycle road racing with long‑term goals of the Isle of Man TT, while building toward professional car programs in Porsche Cup, SRO GT3, and IMSA. With a dual focus on two and four wheels, we represent speed, innovation, and performance.
